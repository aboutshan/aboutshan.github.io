Charged Defects Modelling

Much is expected from this relatively young field of physics, given the rapid development of scientific computation and an abundance of novel tools to possibly reshape our fundamental strategies.

Our work (literature review) The modeling of point defects has become an invaluable topic for it may further help us understand the properties of materials. However, first-principle calculations of charged defects using periodic supercells are plagued by the spurious long-range Coulomb interactions between the periodic images of the defect. Several correction schemes are reviewed, with a special focus on the surface/interface or 2D material conditions.
